Account Deletion Instructions

At Diamond Vyapari Book, we value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to delete your account:

  • Method 1 (Within the App): Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  • Method 2 (Via Email): Send an email to your-email@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". Please mention your registered mobile number or email.

What data will be deleted?

Once your request is processed, the following data will be permanently removed from our Firebase servers:

  • Your profile information (Name, Email, Phone Number).
  • All diamond transaction records and business data.
  • Your authentication credentials.

Note: It may take up to 7 business days to complete the deletion process.
